🔒 Strong Passwords

You need to make a new password for your email. Which password is the safest?
❌
Password A: WEAK
123456
Only numbers, too short, easy to guess
⚠️
Password B: OKAY
password
Real word, but everyone knows it and no numbers
✅
Password C: STRONG
MyDog2024!
Has letters, numbers, and symbols - hard to guess!
💡
Remember: Good Passwords Keep You Safe!
✅ At least 8 letters long
✅ Big and small letters
✅ Numbers
✅ Special symbols (like ! or @)
✅ Not a word everyone knows or your name
❌ Password A: "123456" - easy to remember
⚠️ Password B: "password" - it's a word
✅ Password C: "MyDog2024!" - has letters, numbers, and symbols

Remember: When in doubt, ask for help! 💙
