Question 5

📸 Sharing Photos

Your friend wants to post a picture of you both at the mall. What should you think about?
📱
Sharing Pictures
"Hey! I took a great picture of us at the mall today. I want to post it and say where we were. You look great!"
THINGS TO WORRY ABOUT:
• Shows where you were • Your picture shared without asking • Stays on the internet forever
💭
Remember: You Choose About Your Pictures!

You get to decide if your pictures can be shared online.

👍 Say "Sure, go ahead and post it!"
🤔 Ask them to post it but don't say where we were
🚫 Ask them not to post it because I want to keep it private

Remember: When in doubt, ask for help! 💙
